DCMA Takedown Policy

Step 1 : Copyright Owner Investigates.

A copyright owner should always conduct an initial investigation to confirm both (a) that they own the copyright to an original work and (b) that the content on Reach For The Stars is unauthorized and infringing.

Step 2 : Copyright Owner Sends A Notice

After conducting an investigation, a copyright owner prepares and sends a takedown notice to Reach For The Stars. Assuming the takedown notice is sufficiently detailed, we will pass the notice along to the affected user via email.

Step 3 : Asks User to Make Changes

We will contact the user who created the collection and give them approximately 24 hours to delete or modify the content specified in the notice. We'll notify the copyright owner if and when we give the user a chance to make changes.
